About

I serve as a Technology Lead and Systems Administrator for a 300-student public charter school, responsible for identity management, endpoint administration, Google Workspace governance, security enforcement, vendor coordination, and infrastructure planning.

My role extends beyond support into infrastructure ownership, including network segmentation design, VLAN planning, risk audits, and modernization initiatives aligned to long-term operational stability.

Infrastructure Engineering & Automation

I design systems with clear separation of compute, storage, networking, and identity. Recent work includes containerized workloads, persistent NFS-backed storage architecture, and a Golden Image Factory built with Packer, Cloud-Init, Ansible, and AWS AMI publishing.

My engineering approach emphasizes immutable infrastructure, versioned artifacts, SSH hardening, scoped IAM usage, and elimination of unmanaged root access.

Observability & Reliability

I implement observability as part of system design, not as an afterthought. My monitoring stack includes Prometheus, Node Exporter, Grafana dashboards, and Alertmanager with structured Slack routing.

  • Service health validation and failure detection
  • Metric aggregation and dashboard-driven diagnostics
  • Automated restart workflows and operational safeguards
  • Lifecycle cleanup and rollback discipline
